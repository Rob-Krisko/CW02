Exercise 6

The school can use a variety of different styling options in order to make their collective pages look unique, while still maintaining the overall look.
Some Examples they could use would be:

  • Utilizing Floating or fixed boxes within their respecitve page
  • Utilizing either 'ul' or 'ol' to make ordered and unordered lists
  • Using 'span' attributes to change and add emphasis onto certain words or phrases
  • Use the table attribute to insert tables, both with and without borders
  • Utilizing color to change the font colors within the page or sections
  • Use background color to make different sections have a different background color
  • Use the img tag to place relevant images within their page
  • They can add their own imbedded styling within the header that should coincide with the overall styling provided by the school
  • They could add in links to relevant outside information, such as videos
  • They could style specific sections within by assigning classes to individual items within the page
